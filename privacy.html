Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 23 July 2026

ScanWise scans food and cosmetic products and shows you a transparent health score. Your health profile is deeply personal, and this policy explains exactly what stays on your device, what reaches our servers, and what becomes public. Where we say something is not stored, we mean it literally.

1. Information Stored on Your Device

The following is stored only in your device's local storage and is never sent to our servers:

  • Your body profile (weight, height, age, sex).
  • Your dietary preferences, cosmetic sensitivities, and allergens.
  • Your scan history (Journal) and favorited products.
  • Your personal Google Gemini API key, if you choose to provide one.

2. Your Account

ScanWise creates an anonymous account for your device the first time you open the app. It has no name, email address, or any other identifying detail — it exists so that we can apply fair usage limits to AI features and attribute contributions to the shared catalog. You do not sign up for it and you cannot be identified by it.

You may optionally upgrade to a permanent account using an email address, or by signing in with Google or Apple. If you do:

  • We store your email address, and for Google or Apple sign-in, the account identifier and email that provider shares with us. We do not receive your password.
  • Your existing anonymous account is upgraded in place, so activity already linked to your device remains linked to you.
  • Your on-device health profile stays on your device. Creating an account does not upload it.

3. How We Process External Data

  • Public databases: When you scan a barcode, the barcode number is sent to Open Food Facts, Open Beauty Facts, or USDA to retrieve ingredients and nutrition data.
  • AI insights: When you request an AI "Wise Note", the product's ingredients and relevant parts of your profile are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate the note. Google processes this as our service provider and does not use it to train its models.
  • Label reading: When you photograph an ingredients label for the AI to read, the image is sent for text extraction and then discarded — it is never saved. Only the extracted text is kept.

4. Contributions to the Public Catalog

This section describes the only data you can make public, so please read it before contributing.

If you add a product that is missing from our sources, or improve an existing entry, the product details you confirm — name, brand, ingredients, and nutrition values — are saved to a shared catalog visible to all ScanWise users, keyed to the product's barcode.

If you also take a separate front-of-pack product photo, that photo is uploaded to public storage and becomes the catalog image for that product. It is publicly accessible to anyone with the link. This is deliberate and distinct from the label photo described above, which is never stored. Please photograph only the product packaging — never yourself, other people, or your surroundings.

Contributions are published without your name or email attached, though they remain linked to your account internally so we can review and correct submissions.

5. Analytics and Usage Data

We record basic product usage events — app opened, product scanned, a view changed, an AI note viewed or failed — to understand what works and what breaks. These events are linked to your account identifier. They do not contain your health profile, your body measurements, or the contents of your journal, and we do not use them to build a profile of you or share them with advertisers.

We also record a count of your AI requests in order to apply usage limits and monitor costs.

6. Legal Basis and Retention

Where the GDPR applies, we process account and usage data on the basis of our legitimate interest in operating and improving the app, and process contributions on the basis of your consent, given by submitting them. Account and usage data are kept until you ask us to delete them. Catalog contributions may be retained after account deletion, since other users rely on them, but they are not identifiable as yours.

7. Your Rights

You can delete everything held on your device at any time via "Clear scan journal" in Settings.

For data held on our servers — your account, your usage events, and your contributions — you may request a copy or ask us to delete it by contacting us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days. If you are in the EU or UK, you also have the right to object to processing and to complain to your national data protection authority.

8. Children

ScanWise is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you within the app.

Contact: For any privacy question or request, email support@scanwise.app.
